Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to what most customers ask us first.

Pricing is based on the volume of items removed and the type of materials. We provide a free, no-obligation on-site estimate before any work begins. The final price is quoted and approved by you before we start loading — so there are never any surprise charges.
For safety and to answer questions, we ask that someone 18 or older be present when we arrive to review the estimate and authorize the work. Once you approve, you’re welcome to go about your day — we handle everything from there.
We cannot haul hazardous waste including paint, chemicals, pesticides, asbestos, propane tanks, or medical waste. For a complete list, see our Services page or contact us directly — we’re happy to help point you to the right resources for those items.
We operate by appointment only, so the sooner you reach out, the better. In many cases we can schedule within a few days of your request. Contact us through our form and we’ll find a time that works for you.
When possible, we separate items that can be donated or recycled to keep usable goods and materials out of Hawaii’s landfills. Hawaii has strict e-waste regulations, and we’re committed to handling electronics responsibly. We believe in responsible hauling as part of caring for our island.
