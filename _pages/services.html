Important to Know

What We Cannot Remove

For safety and legal reasons, certain materials require specialized disposal that we’re not equipped to handle.

Prohibited Items

We are unable to haul hazardous or regulated materials. If you have items on this list, please contact the City & County of Honolulu’s Refuse Division or a licensed hazardous waste handler.

  • Paint & Solvents
  • Pesticides & Herbicides
  • Asbestos-containing materials
  • Propane & fuel tanks
  • Motor oil & automotive fluids
  • Medical waste
  • Biological / biohazard materials
  • Explosives or ammunition
  • Flammable liquids
  • Uncontained chemicals

Not sure if your item qualifies? Contact us and we’ll let you know before you book.
